# GREST Warranty Policy and FAQ

## Warranty Overview - What warranty do you offer? / kitni warranty milti hai?

Every iPhone and MacBook you buy from GREST comes with a 6 + 6 month warranty for complete peace of mind.

## Warranty Duration / How long is the warranty period?

- Most GREST devices come with a 6-month warranty, starting from your purchase date
- Extended warranty available: You can purchase an extended warranty for additional 6 months at Rs. 1,499
- Total potential coverage: 12 months (6 months standard + 6 months extended)

## What does the warranty cover?

Our warranty covers:
- Software issues
- Functional problems like speaker, microphone, and performance glitches
- Hardware issues due to manufacturing defects

## What is NOT covered under warranty?

The following are NOT covered:
- Physical damage
- Display damage  
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repairs
- Accessories (chargers and cables) are covered only for manufacturing defects

You can raise a warranty request by:

## Is warranty valid across India?

Yes! No matter where you are in India, your warranty remains valid. We provide nationwide support with hassle-free service.

## Can I extend my warranty?

Yes, you can purchase an extended warranty for 6 months at Rs. 1,499 at the time of buying your iPhone. This gives you even longer protection and peace of mind.
