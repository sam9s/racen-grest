One-to-One Sessions with Shaveta - How to Get Started

If you're interested in working directly with Shaveta in a personalized one-to-one session, the first step is to book a complimentary Discovery Call. This is a 15-minute call designed to help you gain clarity on whether working together is the right fit for your journey.

To book your Discovery Call, visit: https://bit.ly/apply-for-discovery

Discovery Call FAQs:

What should I expect before my call?
After you book your time, you'll receive a confirmation email with your session details. Add it to your calendar so it's locked in. You will also receive email reminders before your call.

How should I prepare for my call?
Take a few moments to ground yourself before the session. Find a quiet space, bring a notebook if you'd like, and come with openness and honesty. This is a sacred space for exploration and clarity. Just come as you are. You'll be guided through everything during the call.

What happens after my call?
After your Discovery Call, you'll have the clarity you need to decide whether working with Shaveta feels aligned. You may be invited into a personalized program, or you might simply walk away with guidance to support your next steps. Either way, you'll leave with more awareness and direction for your journey.

How long is the Discovery Call?
The call typically lasts around 15 minutes.

Is there a cost to book?
No, this Discovery Call is complimentary for those who feel ready to explore working with Shaveta.

What if I can't find a time that works?
You can reach out via email through our contact page at https://www.joveheal.com/contact, and we'll do our best to find a time that fits your schedule.

Who is the Discovery Call for?
The Discovery Call is perfect for anyone who:
- Wants to work directly with Shaveta in a one-to-one setting
- Is considering personal coaching or private sessions
- Wants to explore if JoveHeal's personalized approach is right for them
- Is ready to take the next step in their healing and transformation journey
